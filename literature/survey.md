# Literature Survey

## CODA / CODA-LM

- Role in this repo: primary implemented corner-case replay benchmark.
- Relevance: validates open-world, corner-case perception and structured QA in
  degraded visual environments.

## DTPQA

- Role in this repo: missing benchmark integration targeted in the current
  implementation cycle.
- Relevance: measures distance-aware traffic perception QA, especially useful for
  small/compact VLM degradation analysis at longer ranges.

## DriveLM

- Role in this repo: planned but not yet implemented benchmark for graph VQA and
  perception-to-decision alignment.
- Relevance: important for later-stage evaluation, but secondary to completing
  DTPQA first because DTPQA is a narrower and more directly actionable gap.
